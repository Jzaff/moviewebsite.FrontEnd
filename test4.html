How to make Swiss roll cake / Basic roll cake recipe / Easy roll cake

Materials

▶ Frame Size: width 39cm, width 29cm, height 4.5cm
5 egg yolks
Salt 1g
Sugar 50g
Flour 90g
Baking Powder 2g
Vegetable oil 50g
Milk 50g
Meringue (5 egg whites + Sugar 70g)

Bake at 170C for about 20 minutes
